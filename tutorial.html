Gameplay

  • Avoid the chainsaws and collect as many stars as possible. See what is the highest score you can reach!
  • You'll start with three lives, every time you get hit by a chainsaw you take a step back and lose one heart.
  • Collect hearts in order to restore lives.
  • When a chainsaw hit you, you acquire temporary invulnerability.
  • When you become invulnerable, you cannot lose life but you cannot collect stars and hearts either.

Commands

  • Press ESC to pause the game.
  • Press SPACE to jump.
  • Press 1,2 or 3 to change the camera position.
  • Press 4 to lock/unlock the camera. Once unlocked, you can control it by pressing the left click and moving it.
  • Press A or ← to dash left.
  • Press D or → to dash right.
